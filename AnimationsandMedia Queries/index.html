About us

Welcome to WTL Company, your trusted partner in transforming your house into a home. Based in the heart of the community, we've dedicated ourselves to assisting homeowners through every step of their home ownership journey. Our name, WTL, stands for We Transform Lives, a commitment that lies at the core of everything we do.

Our mission

At WTL Company, we believe that your home should be a reflection of your dreams, needs, and the life you wish to live. Our mission is to empower homeowners with the knowledge, resources, and services required to make informed decisions and realize their home's full potential. Whether you're navigating the complexities of home ownership for the first time or looking to make your current space more enjoyable, we're here to guide you.

Eco-Friendly Home Renovation

Overview: The Green Haven Project was an ambitious undertaking to transform a traditional 20th-century home into a model of sustainability and modern living. Our objective was not only to update the aesthetics and functionality of the space but also to significantly reduce its environmental footprint.

Services Provided:

  • Comprehensive energy audit and retrofitting with energy-efficient solutions, including solar panels and smart home technology.
  • Installation of low-flow water fixtures and a rainwater harvesting system to promote water conservation.
  • Use of sustainable and locally sourced materials for construction and finishes.
